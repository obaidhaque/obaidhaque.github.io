Photography was an outlet for me to design and execute creative visions.

My work was featured on: Complex, END Clothing, Hypebeast, Highsnobiety, Kixify, and Montreal Gazette.

Throughout my work, I have collaborated with clients, designed sets, executed shots, and edited revisions to produce visuals on a high level.



This project was shot in New York City for Adidas Canada.

For this shoot, I secured a rooftop location in the Lower East Side. I collaborated with @thebrightesthour to design and execute the shots. Revisions were edited in Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Lightroom to produce the final image.
